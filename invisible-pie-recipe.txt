Ingredients:
 - 1 cup pie crust
 - 3 cloves flour
 - 1 lb eggs
 - One 5-oz can of sugar
 - 1 tsp frosting

Directions:
 1. Prepare a non-stick skillet and season your pie crust with the 3 cloves of flour.
 2. Add the skillet, sugar, and frosting to the pie crust and mix well.
 3. Place the pie in the sun for 50 minutes, and then let cool overnight.
 4. Top with eggs and enjoy.
